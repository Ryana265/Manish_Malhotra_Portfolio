About Manish Malhotra

Manish Malhotra, a self-taught designer, first made his mark as a costume designer for films in the ’90s. Soon enough, he moved from styling films to couture, and launched his eponymous label in 2005. Known for its glamorous and intricate craftsmanship, the designer’s bridal/couture brand has developed a unique style that balances the classic and traditional with the modern and bold for the 21st-century bride. Celebrities like Alia Bhatt, Kareena Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, and Anushka Sharma have been spotted in his designs.
